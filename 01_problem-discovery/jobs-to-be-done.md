# Jobs To Be Done

## Persona 1: The Daily Student Commuter
- **When I** check the schedule before leaving for class  
- **I want** to see if the bus is crowded in real time  
- **So I can** decide whether to wait for the next bus or take an alternative route  

## Persona 2: The Parent with Child
- **When I** need to travel with my toddler and stroller  
- **I want** accurate crowding information before boarding  
- **So I can** ensure a safe and comfortable ride for both of us  

## Persona 3: The Multi-line Commuter
- **When I** transfer between two or more lines during rush hour  
- **I want** reliable crowding data across all lines  
- **So I can** minimize delays and reduce the stress of switching vehicles
