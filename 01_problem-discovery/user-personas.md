# User Personas

## Persona 1: The Daily Student Commuter
- **Demographics:** 22 years old, university student, lives in Tel Aviv, commutes daily by bus/train.
- **Goals:** Arrive on time to classes, avoid crowded rides, minimize stress.
- **Pain Points:** Often stuck in overcrowded buses, unreliable crowding information, feels reporting is a hassle.
- **Motivations:** Wants quick, reliable info to plan trips; values comfort and predictability.
- **Quote:** "I just want to know if the bus will be packed before I get on."

## Persona 2: The Parent with Child
- **Demographics:** 35 years old, parent of a toddler, uses buses for daycare drop-off.
- **Goals:** Travel safely and comfortably with child, avoid standing in crowded vehicles.
- **Pain Points:** Difficult to manage stroller in crowded buses, stressful when reports are missing.
- **Motivations:** Needs trustworthy crowding info to plan trips around child’s comfort.
- **Quote:** "If I know the bus is full, I’ll wait for the next one."

## Persona 3: The Multi-line Commuter
- **Demographics:** 40 years old, office worker, switches 2–3 lines daily.
- **Goals:** Optimize travel time, reduce delays, avoid overcrowding during transfers.
- **Pain Points:** Reports are inconsistent between lines, wastes time waiting for less crowded options.
- **Motivations:** Wants reliable, real-time data to make quick decisions.
- **Quote:** "Switching lines is stressful enough — I need accurate info to make it smoother."
